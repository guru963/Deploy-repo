What is AltiCred AI?

AltiCred AI is a new-age credit scoring model designed for inclusive lending. By analyzing alternative data, it provides a personalized, explainable credit score to determine the probability of default for those without formal credit histories.

Our Unique Scoring Metrics

Digital Trust Graph Score

Analyzes social network proximity to defaulters and reliable contacts for a trust-based evaluation.

Resilience Recovery Score

Measures how quickly an individual bounces back financially after a crisis based on historical patterns.

Adaptability Score

Evaluates how well an individual adjusts to financial shifts, promoting a forward-looking credit view.

Language Sentiment Score

Detects financial optimism or distress by analyzing the tone of consented social content.

Our Impact in the Modern World

In a world where millions are still underbanked, AltiCred AI provides a crucial pathway to financial inclusion.
